About

 

CREDAI

Since 1998, India’s renowned association CREDAI– Nagpur Metro has been effortlessly involved in eradicating the problems and obstacles in the path of working of the builders and promotion of their activities.


By representing the builders at the national level through affiliation to Maharashtra and national-level bodies, CREDAI- Nagpur Metro has become the No. 1 trusted Association for the Indian real estate industry. CREDAI being the voice of thousands of members contributes towards bringing more transparency to the sector through its efforts.


India’s apex body for private real estate developers, The Confederation of real estate developers association of India (CREDAI) is representing over 9000 developers through 20 states and 136 cities across the country
